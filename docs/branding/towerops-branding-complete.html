07 — Brand Voice
Voice & Messaging
Specific. Measurable. No filler. Built for people who make decisions in the field.
Precise
"WO-1047 breached SLA by 2h 14m. Mahmoud checked in 3.2km from site."
No ambiguity. Exact data. Immediately actionable. Every statement is verifiable against a record in the system.
Direct
"Visit incomplete. 6 photos missing. Cannot submit."
No softening. No padding. Engineers on site need total clarity. Politeness is for the client portal, not the field checklist.
Reliable
"12 records synced. 1 conflict resolved. All data secured."
The system always tells you what happened. No silent failures. Every action has a confirmation. Trust is built through accountability.
✕ Never say this
"Streamline your operations"
"End-to-end field service management"
"Transform your telecom workflows"
"Industry-leading platform"
"Powerful and intuitive"
✓ Say this instead
"Cut SLA breach rate by 40% in 90 days"
"GPS-verified evidence that wins SLA disputes"
"Deploy in 7 days. No consultants."
"Built for tower maintenance. Not configured for it."
"The only FSM platform in Arabic and English"
